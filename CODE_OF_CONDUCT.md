# Code of Conduct

License: MIT (see LICENSE).

## Our standards
Participants are expected to:
- be respectful and constructive
- give actionable technical feedback
- assume good intent while discussing disagreements
- avoid harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks

## Unacceptable behavior
- abusive or demeaning language
- threats or intimidation
- doxxing or sharing private information without consent
- spam or deliberately disruptive conduct

## Scope
This code of conduct applies in project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and other community channels tied to this repository.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or block contributors for behavior that violates this policy.
